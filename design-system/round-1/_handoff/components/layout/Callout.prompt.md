**Callout** — full-width CTA band; the conversion moment on marketing pages.

```jsx
<Callout eyebrow="Ready to cook?" title="Your 200-page PDF deserves better than a download button." primaryLabel="Contact the Kitchen" secondaryLabel="See our work">Tell us about your report and we'll show you what's possible.</Callout>
```

tones: rhino (dark), tomato, cream. `whisk` shows the sanctioned corner accent.
