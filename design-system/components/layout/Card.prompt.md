**Card** — the Our Work project tile with a photo-free color-block cover.

```jsx
<Card client="Enterprise Community Partners" title="Housing2Justice National Toolkit" description="Helping the housing industry serve the justice-impacted population." coverColor="var(--rk-rhino-700)" coverIcon="scale" coverText="Toolkit" tags={["Housing","Toolkit"]} />
```

Cover is a flat color block with a large Lucide icon — no photography. Hover lifts the card and turns the title tomato.
