## What we read
- **What it is:** A research-grounded report from the Learning Policy Institute and EdPrepLab laying out five design principles for teacher preparation programs built on the science of learning and development (SoLD).
- **Audience:** Teacher educators, preparation program leaders, accrediting/licensing bodies, and education policymakers.
- **Core message:** Teacher preparation should be redesigned around how people actually learn and develop — grounding candidates in deep knowledge of learners, equitable mindsets, experiential learning, pedagogical modeling, and communities of practice.
